Attestation of Company Good Standing (ACGS)

ACGS is a document issued by SSM to confirm the existence of a company and that it has met the statutory requirements by the Companies Act 2016 (Act 777). Below are the criteria for the requirements of ACGS to a company:

  • The company has been incorporated for at least 18 months from the date of the ACGS;
  • The company has lodged its latest Annual Return and Audited Financial Statements or its latest Exempt Private Company (EPC) certificate;
  • The company’s status is ‘existing’ and is not in the process of being wound or struck off or dissolved;
  • The company is not dormant as per nature of business;
  • The company has a registered address;
  • The company or its directors do not have any outstanding compound with SSM; and
  • The company or its directors do not have pending prosecution case with SSM.
