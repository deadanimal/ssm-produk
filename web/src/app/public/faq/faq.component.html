HOW CAN WE HELP?

XCESS Portal is an SSM Product Portal that provides online services to supply company and business information registered with SSM.

The XCESS portal can be used on 1 January 2021.

(a) Open the internet browser and type https://ssm4u.com.my.

(b) Click “REGISTER” to register and obtain your user ID.

(c) Click “SIGN IN” and key in the user ID and password if the user is a registered user of SSM4U.

(d) Click icon “XCESS PORTAL”.

Skrin Portal XCESS akan dipaparkan.

Documents and products available are:

(a) Comapany and Business Profile

(b) Statutory Document Forms & Images

(c) Financial Historical/Comparison

(d) Certificate of Incorporation/Registration

(e) Particular of Directors/Officers

(f) Particular of Share Holders

(g) Particular of Registered Address

(h) Particular Change of Name

(i) Particular of Conversion

(j) Particular of Company Secretary

(k) Audit Firm Profile

(l) Company Listing (Bulk Purchase)

(m) Personal Involvement

Customers can refer to the Price Schedule available in the XCESS Portal.

The price is based on the Companies Regulations 2017, Companies Act 2016.

Customers can refer to the XCESS Portal manual which can be found on the XCESS Portal.

Customers may contact SSM Contact Centre at 03-77214000 / fax 03-77214001 / email to enquiry@ssm.com.my  for further explanation.

SSM Product Portal is best viewed using Chrome latest version, Mozilla latest version and Internet Explorer latest version.

Refund application is only allowed if there is an error of information or data on the document / product that has been purchased.

Any inquiries and complaints on this matter should be submitted to enquiry@ssm.com.my   for the further action.
